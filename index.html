About My Dog: Wilma

My Dog Wilma is the greates dog that I've ever had. My family originally got her for my dad because he had lost his dog, Willy a few years back (hence her name being Wilma) but when we got her she for some reason got very attached to me. She has been my best friend and has been by my side for years. She is the sweetest dog I think anyone will ever meet and I will always love her.
